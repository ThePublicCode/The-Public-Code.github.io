1. **Describe what's frustrating you**

   You don't need to know what the solution is — that's my job. Just tell me what's slow, broken, or taking up too much of your team's time. Plain language is fine.

2. **I'll figure out what's needed**

   I'll get back to you and ask a few questions to understand what's actually going on. A lot of the time the technical solution is simpler than people expect — I'll explain it in plain terms.

3. **It gets built**

   I'll build it, or connect you with an engineer I trust. Either way, you'll know exactly who's working on it before anything starts.
